The Moment I Fell in Love with Thailand

The Food, The Islands, and the Smiles

Literally right before the world shut down, due to Covid, my friends and I had the pleasure of vacationing in Thailand. We visited Bangkok for a couple of days, where we toured the beautiful temples and ate some of my favorite Thai dishes. We also flew down to Phuket and stayed a couple of days there as well. Where we island hopped the beautiful Phi Phi Islands. What a breathtaking site! Here is a list of some activities during the trip.

  • Temple Tour, Bangkok
  • Fly to Phuket
  • Go to Bangla Road
  • Island Hop Phi Phi Island
  • Eat Well!
